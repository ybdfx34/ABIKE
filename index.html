My Abike,

Four years ago, on March 12, life quietly gave me one of its greatest gifts—you.

From the little conversations to the unforgettable memories, you’ve become one of the best parts of my life. Every day with you reminds me that love isn’t always loud; sometimes it’s found in the little moments, the random laughs, the silent prayers, and simply knowing you’re there.

If I Could Describe You…

You truly are my African Queen—out of a million, you stand as one.
